Research Connect

I worked in a team to build Research Connect as part of a school organization. The site showed students what research opportunities were available at our school, and allowed Professors to post opportunities.

I served as the Product Manager for this project but also wrote a significant amount of front-end and back-end code. (The club's Developer Leads even labelled me a "problem child" once because I wrote too much code as a PM.) We used MongoDB, Express, React, Node, and Typescript.
