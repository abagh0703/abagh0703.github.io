Treatment Without Borders

The FB Global Finals hackathon brought together teams from around the world, where each team (including ours!) had won a hackathon in their region to qualify for this hackathon. We built a React Native app to help people in under-developed regions identify symptoms of common life-threatening illnesses in that region with the help of doctors who volunteered their time.

I worked on the back end for the app using NodeJS, Express, and Firebase. The pitch deck we used was on one of our school emails and it got deleted once they graduated, so no product images unfortunately.
