Retail Trail

Retail Trail was a website that showed users the inventory of retail stores near them. For example, if a user bought tuna but doesn't have a can opener and really wanted to eat that tuna, they could search Retail Trail to see which retails stores near them have it and then go buy it from that store. That way they wouldn't have to wait two days for it to arrive if ordered online or go to a 7/11 only to find out they don't have can openers.

This was made during a hackathon with a friend. I worked on the front-end and some of the back-end. We used Flask and PostgreSQL for the back end + database respectively.
