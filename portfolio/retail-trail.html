Retail Trail

Retail Trail was a website that showed users the inventory of retail stores near them. For example, if a user needed a bottle opener, they could search retail trail to see which retails stores near them have it and then go buy it from that store.

This was made during a hackathon with a friend. I worked on the front-end and some of the back-end. We used Flask and PostgreSQL for the back end + database respectively.
