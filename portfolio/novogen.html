Novogen

Novogen.ai is a web platform that empowers scientists with tools to generate novel compounds with desired chemical descriptors. I built it with a team at Stanford's Tree Hacks, and we won the best Data Science in Health award!

I worked on the front end for this using jQuery and helped with a little bit of the back end in Flask.

  • Stack: jQuery, Flask, and Firebase.
